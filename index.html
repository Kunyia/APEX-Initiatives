WHY
"I got into a top university. My best friend — just as smart, just as driven — didn't even know where to apply. The difference wasn't talent. It was access."
— APEX Founder, Class of 2023

In 2022, a group of college students realized the opportunities that changed their lives — test prep, mentorship, application coaching — were invisible to most of the world. Not because students didn't want them. Because they didn't know they existed, or couldn't afford them.

So they built APEX Initiatives. No fees. No gatekeeping. No prerequisites. Just students helping students, across three of the most opportunity-defining fields on earth.

About us

Built by students, for students who deserve more.

APEX Initiatives was founded by a group of university students who noticed a pattern: the resources that changed their lives — prep courses, consulting, mentorship — were invisible to most of the world.

We're a fully student-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Every mentor is a current college student or recent graduate. No corporate gatekeeping. No fees. No compromise.

⚡
Peer-to-peer, always
Students trust students. Every mentor has lived the experience they're sharing — no corporate consultants.
🌍
Global by design
From Nigeria to Nicaragua, rural Texas to rural India — if you have wifi and ambition, you're welcome here.
🔓
Radical access
Free means free. No freemium, no upsells, no premium tier. Every student gets identical quality of support.
🔄
The flywheel
Students we help become mentors who help the next wave. The movement compounds itself.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

APEX Initiatives is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organisation based in the United States. When we say "APEX", "we", "us", or "our" in this policy, we mean APEX Initiatives. You can reach us at hello@apexinitiatives.org.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide when you fill out a form on this site: your name, email address, country or location, program interest, and any optional fields such as your current SAT score or a short message about why you want to volunteer.

3. How we use your data

We use your data solely to match you with a program, contact you about your application or request, and send occasional updates about APEX. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any commercial purpose.

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II certified cloud database hosted in the United States. All connections are encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). Access is restricted to APEX leadership only.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party, except as required by law or to operate core services (e.g. our database provider). We never share data with advertisers.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Simply email hello@apexinitiatives.org with the subject line "Data Request" and we will respond within 14 days.

7. Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. No third-party advertising or analytics scripts are loaded.

8. Changes

If we update this policy materially, we will update the date above and, where possible, notify users by email.
