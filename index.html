WHY

"I got into a top university. My best friend — just as smart, just as driven — didn't even know where to apply. The difference wasn't talent. It was access."

— APEX Founder, Class of 2023

In 2022, a group of college students realized the opportunities that changed their lives — test prep, mentorship, application coaching — were invisible to most of the world. Not because students didn't want them. Because they didn't know they existed, or couldn't afford them.

So they built APEX Initiatives. No fees. No gatekeeping. No prerequisites. Just students helping students, in three of the most opportunity-defining fields on earth.

Why we exist

Talent is everywhere.
Opportunity is not.
We're here to fix that.


Test prep companies charge $150/hour. College consultants charge $5,000 a package. Medical school advisors cost thousands. These barriers don't reflect who deserves access — just who can afford it.

APEX exists because the gap between knowing and not knowing something changes entire life trajectories. We close that gap permanently, freely, globally.

About us

Built by students,
for students who deserve more.

APEX Initiatives was founded by a group of university students who noticed a pattern: the resources that changed their lives — prep courses, consulting, mentorship — were invisible to most of the world.

We're a fully student-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Every mentor is a current college student or recent graduate who's been exactly where our students are. No corporate gatekeeping. No fees. No compromise.

⚡
Peer-to-peer, always
Students trust students. We hire no corporate consultants — every mentor has lived the experience they're sharing.
🌍
Global by design
From Nigeria to Nicaragua, rural Texas to rural India — if you have wifi and ambition, you're welcome here.
🔓
Radical access
Free means free. No "freemium." No upsells. No premium tier. Every student gets the exact same quality of support.
🔄
The flywheel
Students we help become mentors who help the next wave. The movement compounds itself.
