Free mentorship in college prep, healthcare, and business.

APEX Initiatives pairs students with volunteer mentors for one-on-one support. There's no cost and no application fee.

Every program is free. We don't charge students at any point.

"I got into a school I was proud of. A friend just as capable didn't know where to start. The difference wasn't ability — it was access to information." — APEX founder
Why we exist

Test prep, consulting, and mentorship shouldn't depend on what your family can afford.

APEX was started by students who noticed that the resources that helped them most — structured test prep, someone to review an essay, a mentor who'd already been through it — were things most people never hear about, let alone pay for.

We run three programs, all volunteer-led and all free. If something's unclear about how any of it works, — we're a small team and we read every message.

Volunteer

Mentor a student

If you're in college, or recently were, you can help a younger student figure out something you once had to figure out yourself. We ask for a regular time commitment, not a big one.

  • Subject mentor
    One-on-one academic tutoring and coaching.
  • College consultant
    Essay review and application strategy.
  • Workshop facilitator
    Lead group sessions and webinars.
  • Operations & outreach
    Help run things behind the scenes.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

APEX Initiatives is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in the United States. "APEX," "we," or "us" in this policy refers to APEX Initiatives. Contact us at hello@apexinitiatives.org.

2. What we collect

Only what you give us through a form on this site: name, email, country or location, program interest, and optional fields like your SAT score or why you want to volunteer.

3. How we use it

To match you with a program, follow up on your application, and send occasional updates about APEX. We don't use your data for advertising or analytics profiling.

4. Storage and security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a SOC 2 Type II certified database provider. Connections are encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is limited to APEX leadership.

5. Sharing

We don't sell or rent your data. We don't share it with third parties except where required by law or to operate the database itself.

6. Your rights

You can access, correct, or request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@apexinitiatives.org with the subject "Data Request." We respond within 14 days.

7. Cookies

This site doesn't use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

8. Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date above and notify users by email where possible.
